Do tortoise eat?

Tortoises like a variety of plant-based
foods. Produce: Depending on the species
of your tortoise, about 80% of your pet
tortoise's daily diet should be fresh
vegetables like kale, dandelions, mustard
and collard greens. For fun, toss in a little
green or yellow bell pepper, sweet potato,
squash or cauliflower.
